Orange Bread

Source: by Xiaolong Wong on Unsplash

NOTE: This blast of citrus flavor brings a piece of summer no matter the time of year!

Yield: 14 one-pound loaves

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts chopped orange, rind
  • 6 quarts flour, (6 pounds)
  • 6 tablespoons baking powder, (2 1/4 ounces)
  • 2 teaspoons salt, (1/2 ounce)
  • 2 quarts sugar, (4 pounds)
  • 16 eggs
  • 1 quart evaporated milk
  • 1 quart orange juice
  • 1/2 cup butter, (1/4 pound)

Directions

  1. Cook orange rind in 2 1/2 cups water and 1 cup sugar (1/2 pound)
  2. Sift flour, then measure
  3. Resift with baking powder, salt and the 2 quarts sugar
  4. Beat eggs, add milk, orange juice and rind
  5. Combine with dry ingredients
  6. Add melted butter
  7. Pour into greased bread pans and bake in a moderate oven (350Â° F) until brown, about 50 minutes
